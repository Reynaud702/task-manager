How to Use TaskPro

Creating a New Task

  1. Click on the "+ Add Task" button from the dashboard
  2. Fill in the required fields (Task Title and Due Date)
  3. Add optional details if needed
  4. Click "Submit" to save your task

Managing Your Tasks

  • View all your tasks on the Dashboard
  • Click on any task to see complete details
  • Use the Edit button to update task information
  • Check the checkbox to mark a task as complete

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete a task?

You can delete a task by clicking the trash icon next to the task or by opening the task details and clicking the "Delete" button.

Can I recover a deleted task?

Currently, task deletion is permanent. Always confirm before deleting a task.

Why should I use TaskPro?

TaskPro helps you stay organized, meet deadlines, and reduce stress by keeping all your academic responsibilities in one place. It's designed specifically for students.
